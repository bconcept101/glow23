Game Info

Glow 23 Rules

Glow Number

Members choose Glow Numbers from 1 through 23.

Glow Ball

The Glow Ball bonus number runs from 1 through 20.

Daily Draws

Glow 23 runs three daily draws at 12:00 PM, 3:00 PM, and 7:00 PM.

Entry Cutoff

Entries close 10 minutes before each draw begins.

Glow 23 is a community entertainment number game interface.
